Gene expression changes in 8 genes were studied at day 0, day 15 and day 30. The 8 genes that are under study are p53, INK4, PTEN, APC, MADR2, BRCA1 & 2 along with two housekeeping genes namely GAPDH and beta-actin.

Analysis of Whole Exome Sequencing data and a comparison between two somatic variant callers Mutect and Strelka.

Minimal residual disease monitoring in Chronic Myeloid Leukemia (CML) patients.

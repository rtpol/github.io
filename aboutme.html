The Story!

Born and bred in Hyderabad, India.

I’m incredibly judgemental when it comes to food and shoes. I’m huge fan of Formula 1 .. So generally I’m pretty much busy on Saturdays and Sundays starting from mid-March to Dec watching live races (every year). Also love to click pictures using my phone (mobile photographer).. Coming to the other part .. Did my MSc in Molecular Medicine under the supervision Dr.Étienne Audet-Walsh at Université Laval (Quebec City) where I majorly focussed on understanding the prostate cancer (PCa) cell metabolism. After spending two years in Quebec City, I’m not sure if I understood the PCa cell metabolism completely but definitely understood the art of making authentic Italian pizzas :P. In Aug 2020, Moved to Dr.Berman’s lab (Queen's Uni) to pursue my PhD, where my work revolves around identification of biomarkers for better diagnosis of PCa. I’m super interested in applying Machine learning and Artificial Intelligence techniques on biological data to understand and develop novel diagnostic methods or therapies to treat PCa.

Apart from the above mentioned, I love to cook/bake/eat. I feel cooking and science have many similarities. As quoted by an unknown legend "Real cooking is more about following your heart than following recipes". This quote motivates me to do various experiments in kitchen (#fusionfood :P) and in lab as well. To satisfy the chef in me, I work as a part-time "Pizzaolo" at Atomica, Kingston. (I don’t like to brag) but I’m really good at baking/cooking pizzas. Well… just in case if you want to verify the above statement, feel free to visit Atomica on weekends
